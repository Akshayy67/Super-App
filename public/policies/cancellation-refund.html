Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Subscription Cancellation

1.1. How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team at support@super-app.tech.

1.2. Cancellation Timing

  • Monthly Subscriptions: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You will continue to have access until the period ends.
  • Yearly Subscriptions: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You will continue to have access until the period ends.

1.3. Immediate Cancellation

If you wish to stop your subscription immediately, you can cancel and your access will be revoked at the end of the current billing cycle. No partial refunds are provided for unused time within the current billing period.

2. Refund Policy

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. If you are not satisfied with our Service within the first 30 days of your initial subscription, you can request a full refund.

2.1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be available under the following circumstances:

  • New Subscriptions: Full refund within 30 days of initial subscription purchase
  • Service Issues: If the Service is unavailable or non-functional for extended periods (more than 7 consecutive days)
  • Billing Errors: If you were charged incorrectly due to our error
  • Duplicate Charges: If you were charged multiple times for the same subscription

2.2. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be provided for:

  • Subscriptions cancelled after 30 days of purchase
  • Renewal charges (renewals are considered continuations of service)
  • Partial periods (unused time within a billing cycle)
  • Change of mind after the 30-day guarantee period
  • Violation of Terms of Service leading to account termination

2.3. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at support@super-app.tech
  2. Provide your account email and subscription details
  3. State the reason for your refund request
  4. We will review your request within 5-7 business days
  5. If approved, refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 10-14 business days

2.4. Refund Method

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method used for the subscription:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refunded to the card (5-10 business days)
  • UPI: Refunded to the UPI account (3-5 business days)
  • Net Banking: Refunded to the bank account (7-14 business days)
  • Wallets: Refunded to the wallet (3-5 business days)

3. Subscription Renewal

3.1. Auto-Renewal

Monthly and yearly subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled before the renewal date. You will be charged the current subscription price at the time of renewal.

3.2. Renewal Notifications

We will send you email notifications before your subscription renews, giving you the opportunity to cancel if desired.

3.3. Price Changes

If subscription prices increase, we will notify you at least 30 days in advance. You can cancel before the renewal date to avoid the new price.

4. Student Discount Refunds

Student subscriptions follow the same refund policy. If your student status cannot be verified, your subscription may be converted to a regular plan, and any price difference will be charged or refunded accordingly.

5. Enterprise/Institutional Subscriptions

Enterprise subscriptions are governed by separate agreements. Cancellation and refund terms for enterprise customers are specified in their respective contracts.

6. Processing Time

  • Refund Requests: Reviewed within 5-7 business days
  • Refund Processing: 10-14 business days after approval
  • Payment Method Processing: Additional time may be required by your bank or payment provider

7. Disputed Charges

If you notice an unauthorized charge or billing error, please contact us immediately at support@super-app.tech. We will investigate and resolve the issue promptly.
