BUSINESS NAME:
Super Study App

BUSINESS TYPE:
Software as a Service (SaaS) - Educational Technology Platform

BUSINESS DESCRIPTION:
Super Study App is a unified academic ecosystem platform that consolidates 15+ fragmented student productivity tools into a single, integrated application. We provide AI-powered learning assistance, video collaboration, interview preparation analytics, task management, team collaboration, pair programming, and study tools - all in one platform.

PRIMARY PRODUCT/SERVICE:
Subscription-based premium access to our educational technology platform with features including:
- AI-powered tutoring and chat
- HD video meetings and collaboration
- Interview preparation with real-time analytics
- Task and note management systems
- Team collaboration and pair programming tools
- Study tools including flashcards and spaced repetition

TARGET CUSTOMERS:
- University and college students in India
- Graduates preparing for job interviews
- Study groups and academic teams
- Educational institutions (for bulk licenses)

BUSINESS MODEL:
Freemium SaaS (Software as a Service) with monthly and yearly subscription plans:
- Student Plan: ₹50/month (90% discount for verified students)
- Monthly Plan: ₹249/month (standard pricing)
- Yearly Plan: ₹2,399/year (80% discount)

REVENUE MODEL:
Recurring subscription revenue from premium users. Revenue is generated through:
1. Monthly and yearly subscription payments
2. Student discount subscriptions
3. Enterprise/institutional bulk licenses (future)

- ₹2,399 per transaction (yearly subscriptions)
- Average transaction: ₹249
